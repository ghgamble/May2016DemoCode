Inheritance

*'is a' relationships
'has a' relationships

Restaurant HAS A Menu
Plate HAS (some) FoodItems

Dog IS A Mammal
King Charles Cavalier IS A Dog
Whale IS A Mammal

Inheritance is most often used with IS A relationships
Create the inheritance chain top down (General -> Specific)

*** DO NOT USE INHERITANCE JUST FOR THE HELL OF IT ***
Only use inheritance to save yourself writing code

In inheritance there are SUPER and SUB classes
Wizard =>  Object is SUPER
Object =>  Wizard is SUB
